Our Signature Flavours

Indulge in a taste of modern British cuisine, lovingly prepared with the finest seasonal ingredients. Each dish is a celebration of flavour.

The Classic Full English

A hearty start to your day featuring Cumberland sausage, streaky bacon, eggs any style, roasted tomatoes, field mushrooms, and sourdough toast.

Pan-Seared Sea Bass

Flaky sea bass, perfectly seared with crisp skin, served atop a vibrant pea and broad bean risotto, finished with a lemon butter sauce.

Sticky Toffee Pudding

Our warm, intensely rich sticky toffee pudding, drenched in a buttery caramel sauce and served with a dollop of vanilla bean ice cream.

Wild Mushroom Risotto

Creamy Arborio rice folded with an earthy mix of wild mushrooms, Parmesan, and a hint of truffle oil, garnished with fresh parsley.
